How CASA will check your ID


Online ID Check

Most Australian-issued IDs can be verified in real time. All you have to do is enter you ID details and CASA will confirm that your information matches the records of Document Verification Service (DVS).

  • Australian passport
    Current, or expired within the previous two years, but not cancelled
  • Australian citizenship certificate
  • Australian birth certificate
    NSW, Tasmania and Victorian births only

Manual ID Check

Upload a Certified copy and we will check your identification manually. Your ARN will be issued within 5 business days of your application being submitted.

  • Australian birth certificate
    ACT, NT, South Australia, Queensland, Western Australia births only
  • Foreign passport (current)
  • Foreign birth certificate
